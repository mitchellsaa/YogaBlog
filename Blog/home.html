What is SuperConsciousness?

It is the oldest, wisest, most pure form of consciousness as a human being you can ever represent. It is also called Spirit, or knower, seer, witness, that is one, with consciousness.

How do I attain it? -through Yoga

Yoga is the action of being. Being in the now. Not yesterday, not whatever imaginary feeling or thought comes up to your mind. It is BEING in your totality, equaling that which is everything. Yoga means union in its most basic sense.

How else do I attain it?
-through your breath

Your first breath in this lifetime is the ongoing process of evolution within your being. Breathing means living. You are conscious, aware, full of intentions, and being. Your breath is the language of the mind.

The most certain way to SuperConsciousness: Meditation

Go within. Every thought, emotion, and every other distraction in the like, can be foreseen in the mind, conceived there, like a wave in the ocean. Exploring your being from within is the biggest step toward BEING, here, right now.
